FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your stay.

Standard check-in is at 12:00 PM (noon) and check-out is at 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out are available subject to availability. Contact us in advance to arrange this.

Yes! Complimentary hot buffet breakfast is included with all room types. Breakfast is served from 7:00 AM to 10:30 AM daily in our in-house restaurant on the ground floor.

Yes, free secure parking is available for all guests on a first-come, first-served basis. Our parking area is monitored by CCTV 24/7. Please inform us if you have a large vehicle.

Free cancellation up to 24 hours before check-in. Cancellations within 24 hours will incur a one-night charge. No-shows are charged the full booking amount.

We do not allow pets in the hotel rooms. However, we have designated outdoor areas if you are visiting with a pet. Please contact us beforehand for specific arrangements.

Yes! We provide airport transfer services at a nominal fee. Please contact us at least 24 hours before arrival to arrange your pickup. WhatsApp us for instant coordination.

We accept cash, all major credit/debit cards, UPI (PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm), and bank transfers. Online bookings can be secured with a credit card.
